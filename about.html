Our History

2005

Foundation

Cieverose College was established with a single technical program and 50 students in our first semester.

2010

First Accreditation

Received full accreditation from the Technical Education and Skills Development Authority (TESDA).

2015

Campus Expansion

Opened our second building with modern laboratories and computer facilities.

2020

Online Learning

Successfully transitioned to blended learning during the pandemic, serving over 1,000 students remotely.

Present

Continuing Growth

Now offering 12 degree programs and numerous technical courses with over 2,000 enrolled students.

Our Campus

Cieverose College is located in a 5-hectare property with modern facilities including:

  • 8 fully-equipped computer laboratories
  • Technical workshops for hands-on training
  • Library with over 10,000 volumes
  • Auditorium for events and seminars
  • Student lounge and cafeteria
  • Sports facilities including basketball court
