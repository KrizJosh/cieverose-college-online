Welcome to Cieverose College

At Cieverose College, we are committed to nurturing well-rounded individuals through a balanced approach to education that combines rigorous academics with practical life skills development.

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution that provides high quality training, knowledge, skills and services in all areas specially in technical education industry.

Our Mission

To empower youth by providing quality education which centers in the development of totality of a person in physical, emotional, spiritual, intellectual, and life skills necessary to prepare them to be contributory individuals

Our Philosophy

"To serve God is to Serve Others."

SCHOOL GOALS

  • Provide high-quality graduates that meet industry standards
  • Foster holistic student development addressing physical, emotional, spiritual, intellectual, and life skills
  • Empower youth to become responsible, productive, and contributing members of their communities
  • Cultivate a learning environment rooted in strong faith, integrity, and respect for diversity
  • Promote excellence and hard work among students, faculty, and staff
  • Ensure continuous improvement in educational programs and services to maintain leadership in education
  • Encourage love, respect, and understanding within the school community to build a harmonious environment
  • Conduct research and extension services focused on community development

OUR CORE VALUES, OUR SHIELD

S

Strong Faith

A foundation of spiritual strength and moral integrity that guides personal and communal growth. Strong faith nurtures resilience, hope, and ethical decision-making, empowering students to face challenges with confidence and purpose.

H

Hard Work

The commitment to diligence, perseverance, and consistent effort in all academic and personal endeavors. Hard work instills discipline and a strong work ethic, enabling students to achieve excellence and overcome obstacles.

I

Integrity

Upholding honesty, transparency, and ethical behavior in all actions. Integrity fosters trustworthiness and accountability, essential for building character and maintaining a respectful academic and social environment.

E

Excellence

Striving for the highest standards in education, innovation, and service. Excellence motivates continuous improvement, creativity, and the pursuit of quality in all aspects of learning and community engagement.

L

Love

Cultivating compassion, empathy, and genuine care for others. Love promotes harmonious relationships, social responsibility, and a supportive community where everyone feels valued and respected.

D

Diversity & Deference

Valuing the richness of different cultures, ideas, and perspectives while showing respect and humility towards others. Embracing diversity and practicing deference foster inclusivity, collaboration, and mutual understanding in a globalized world.

This SHIELD framework serves as a guiding compass for students, faculty, and staff at Cieverose College, aligning with its mission to empower youth through holistic education and character formation.

Our Senior High School Strands

Choose the path that aligns with your aspirations and career goals

Information and Communication Technology (ICT)

Prepares students for careers in programming, animation, and computer systems servicing. Covers subjects like computer programming, web development, and networking fundamentals.

  • Career paths: Programmer, Web Developer, IT Specialist
  • Specialized subjects: Programming, Animation, Computer Systems Servicing
  • NC II Certification opportunities

Home Economics (HE)

Focuses on livelihood skills in cookery, bread and pastry production, food and beverage services, tourism, and hospitality management.

  • Career paths: Chef, Baker, Hotel/Restaurant Manager
  • Specialized subjects: Cookery, Food and Beverage Services, Tourism
  • Hands-on training in fully equipped kitchen labs

Humanities and Social Sciences (HUMSS)

Designed for students interested in liberal arts, education, journalism, and social sciences. Develops critical thinking and communication skills.

  • Career paths: Teacher, Lawyer, Journalist, Psychologist
  • Specialized subjects: Creative Writing, Social Sciences, Philosophy
  • Strong preparation for college degrees in arts and humanities

Accountancy, Business and Management (ABM)

Focuses on business principles, financial management, and entrepreneurship. Prepares students for careers in business and accountancy.

  • Career paths: Accountant, Entrepreneur, Business Manager
  • Specialized subjects: Business Math, Finance, Marketing
  • Real-world business simulations and case studies

General Academic Strand (GAS)

Designed for students who are undecided on their college path, offering a broad range of subjects across different disciplines.

  • Career paths: College preparation for any degree program
  • Specialized subjects: Humanities, Social Sciences, Business, Science
  • Flexible curriculum for diverse interests
  • Ideal for students exploring multiple career options
  • Strong foundation for liberal arts and sciences
